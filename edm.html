Cloud Migration 101: Exploring Reasons for Transitioning to the Cloud

The cloud has solidified its role as the foremost driving factor propelling the global shift toward digital business. It now serves as the foundational platform for digital giants, constituting over a quarter of the revenues in both IT and business services - a figure projected to reach 50% by the mid-2020s. Businesses, both large and small, now prefer convenient cloud services over restrictive and expensive outsourcing and on-premise solutions. Given its success, many traditional incumbents have embraced a ‘cloud-first’ strategy.
